Goals

I wish to make it the goal of all of BMN to be the greatest at what we do. My goal is to have at least two BMN member-made saves on the first page at the same time, and that whenever someone searches the words Ship, Sea, Weapon or Bomb, they see BMN member-made saves first.

Rules and Trials

  • No trolling of any kind
  • No spamming
  • No abusing of group manager privilegues
  • No inappropiate/hateful/racist posts
  • Don't get you TPT account banned without an adequate reason
  • Don't lie about anything serious or to a Commanding Officer
Probation Punishments

For anything excessively profane, if you got banned for it in TPT, you will be demoted and placed on level 3 probation for 4 weeks, then you will be moved to level 1 probation.
If you get 5 punishments like this, you will be banned from the group.

For any other few-days ban, the Fleet Admiral will ask you for the reason and then ask jacob1 about it. If the reasons don't match, you will be demoted once.
For any unnescessary ban, a trial and a hearing will be convened. This will determine what punishment is given, if any.

Probation Levels
  • Level 1: Light restrictions from group activities, it will not affect your promotion. May go on your record and lasts for one week.
  • Level 2: Medium restrictions from group activities. May affect your promotion during the next Inactive Movement Cycle, and this may go on your record.
    If you receive this and then receive a level 3 probation, you will be discharged. Lasts for 3 weeks.
  • Level 3: Whatever you did to deserve this, it was bad. Heavy restrictions from group activities, no promotion in the next two IMCs, and if you get another level 2 or higher probation, you will be discharged.
    Also, any Admirals Counsel members who are a group manager and get this probation will be changed down to a Member for 2 months. Will go on you record. Lasts for 3 months.
  • Level 4 (waiting list): This essentially means that we are deciding who you are being replaced with. Goodbye.
Trials
For any severe (or at least partially) offense made by an Admiral, a trial will be convened. There shall be a prosecutor, defense attourney, jury of peers (people of the same counsel), testimonials and a judge (the current Fleet Admiral). All people participating in this trial will have their ranks suspended for reasons against biased judgement, except the judge.
Any actual offense made by an Admiral is more serious than that of any other member, obviously. But if an Admiral is being prosecuted, no one below the AC sit-in Commander may post to the thread. Breaking this rule is punishable by discharge.
